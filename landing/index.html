Predict how the market will perceive your idea

Our simulation engine identifies the likely target audience for an idea, then models how different people will interpret, trust, question, and value it before you ship.

  • Predicts which segments will lean in, hesitate, or dismiss the idea
  • Helps you refine messaging, pricing, and positioning around expected perception
